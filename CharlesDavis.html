New Gas Station Opens

Creats Jobs and Easy Access to Gas and Snacks

In the town of Benton, Wisconsin, a brand new Kwik Trip is opening in the middle of town. This is good for the people of Benton because before they would have to go to the next town over to get gas and snacks, and it brings jobs to the people of Benton.This will be a really great thing for the people of Benton

This is a good thing because it will be open 24/7 so there will always be people working. This creates as many as a dozen jobs in town. It will aslo bring revenue into town in the form of sales tax and may help other businesses with potential customers that are just passing through.

It will also help because it offers a convience in getting food, snacks, and basic necessities without having to drive to the next town over. Another benefit is it modernizes our town to some degree.
